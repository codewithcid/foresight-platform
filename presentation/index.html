Team Introduction0:30
The Problem

Marketing reacts. It rarely anticipates — and almost never proves what worked.

  • →Siloed channels. A customer is one person, but email, SMS, app and web each act blind to the others.
  • →Spend by gut, not causal impact. Budgets chase last-click attribution, not who a message will actually move.
  • →"Success" = vanity metrics. Opens and clicks — not proven incremental revenue.

Theme 2 asks for seamless cross-channel experiences with clear measures of success. Foresight is an agent that anticipates, acts across channels, and proves its impact — in rupees.

Prototype Demonstration1:30
Under the hood

Real ML. Real agents. Real proof.

∿ Causal core the proof spine

LightGBM S-learner estimates each customer's treatment effect (CATE). Every prediction is checked against the actual outcome — live calibration, not a one-off score.

◇ Agentic loop

Strategist → Guardrail → Execution → Critic, with a Thompson-sampling contextual bandit and a self-correcting policy that learns per segment.

✦ AI integration

NVIDIA NIM model pool (Qwen3-80B · Llama-3.3-70B · GPT-OSS / Nemotron-120B) with Groq fallback; keyless image generation; a tool-calling Supervisor exposed over MCP.

⤢ Stack & scale

FastAPI + React/Vite/TS over websockets. Plans project to a 5-lakh addressable base — and the same engine runs on any uploaded dataset.
